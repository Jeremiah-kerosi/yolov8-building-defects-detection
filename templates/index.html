About YOLO (You Only Look Once)

YOLO is an efficient and fast algorithm for object detection. It operates in a single shot to detect multiple objects within an image, making it suitable for real-time processing. Unlike traditional two-stage detectors, YOLO simultaneously performs detection and classification, speeding up the process and maintaining high accuracy. YOLO models are widely used in various applications due to their balance of speed and accuracy.
